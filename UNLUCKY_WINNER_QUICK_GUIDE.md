# ⚡ Unlucky Winner Rule - Quick Guide

## The Rule

**If a card has ZERO marked numbers after 12 calls → Automatic Win! 🎉**

## Simple Explanation

```
12 numbers called + 0 numbers marked = UNLUCKY WINNER!
```

## Example

**Card #5:**
```
B   I   N   G   O
2   17  33  51  62
5   21  35  53  66
8   25  FREE 54  73
10  27  37  56  74
13  28  41  58  75
```

**12 Numbers Called:**
B-1, B-3, I-16, I-19, N-31, N-32, G-49, G-50, O-61, O-70, O-71, O-72

**Result:**
- Card #5 has ZERO of these numbers
- After 12 calls → 🎉 **UNLUCKY WINNER!**

## When It Works

✅ **12+ numbers called**
✅ **0 marked numbers** (excluding FREE)
✅ **Card is registered**
✅ **Card is not locked**

## When It Doesn't Work

❌ Less than 12 numbers called
❌ Card has 1+ marked numbers
❌ Card is not registered
❌ Card is locked

## Why This Rule?

- 🍀 **Rewards bad luck** - Consolation prize
- 🎯 **Keeps players engaged** - Hope even with no marks
- 🎉 **Adds excitement** - Unique winning scenario
- ⚖️ **Fair** - Very rare (< 1% chance)

## How to Win as Unlucky Winner

1. **Watch the count** - Track how many numbers called
2. **Check your card** - Do you have zero marks?
3. **Wait for 12** - Must be at least 12 calls
4. **Call BINGO!** - You're the unlucky winner!

## Probability

**Very Rare!** Less than 1% chance

This makes it special and exciting when it happens! 🎊
